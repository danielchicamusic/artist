Biography

Daniel, Telematics Engineer and a true dance floor artist. Based in Madrid (ESP) but originally from Manizales (COL), he has built a solid career in the electronic music scene since his beginnings in 2011. His sound masterfully combines elements of Minimal, House, Tech and Dance Music, delivering high-energy hybrid sets specially designed for clubs and festivals.

Their productions have had a strong impact in Germany, United Kingdom, Netherlands, Spain, Italy and Colombia, reaching the Top 100, Top 10 and Top 5 on Beatport.

In 2021, recognized by the Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp by Aulart, marking the beginning of his international career where he was later able to take his sound to the decks of Ibiza, Barcelona, Madrid.

In Colombia he consolidated his career with great brands and promoters such as Open Doors Techno & House, Techno Night Collective, Station Group, SCENE, ALIB, Booth, Real People, Grood Taste, After Mood, MadRadio, MeLosTomo, ILOVEMIERCOLES among others.
