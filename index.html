BIO

Daniel, Telematics Engineer and a true Dancefloor artist (DJ - Producer). Based in Madrid (ESP) but originally from Manizales (COL), he has built a solid career in the electronic music scene since his beginnings in 2011 as a DJ. His sound masterfully blends elements of Minimal, House, Tech , and Dance Music, delivering high-energy hybrid sets specially designed for clubs and festivals.

He has performed the Warm Up for Seth Troxler, Ilario Alicante, Marco Carola, Sebastien Leger, Matador, Dennis Ferrer, Matthias Tanzmann, Stefano Noferini, Alexis Cabrera, Hito, wAFF, Yaya, Paco Osuna, Kevin Saunderson, Marco Faraone, Dennis Cruz, Stacey Pullen, Alexi Delano, Marko Nastic, Jean Pierre & Jesse Calosso, Rafa Barrios, Hector Couto, Pablo Luque, among others.
Thanks to collectives such as Open Doors Techno & House, Techno Nights Collective, Groove Box, ALIB, Booth, Real People.
His productions have made a strong impact in Germany, the UK, the Netherlands, Spain, and Colombia, reaching the Top 100, Top 10, and Top 5 on Beatport. In 2021, he was recognized by the Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp by Aulart, marking the start of his international career.
