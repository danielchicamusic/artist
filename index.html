BIO

DJ, Producer, and Engineer
Originally from Manizales (COL), and now based in Madrid (ESP), he has built a solid career over the past 13 years, establishing himself as a key figure in the electronic music scene. His sound blends Tech, Minimal House, and Dance Music, delivering high-energy Hybrid Sets tailored for clubs and festivals.

After solidifying his career in Colombia, he expanded his reach to Madrid, Ibiza, and Barcelona, cities that have played a crucial role in shaping his sound and artistic evolution in recent years.

His music has resonated in top venues and festivals, from One Festival (Manizales) to The Zoo Festival (Cali) and GroodTaste (Medellín).

Selected for the Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp 2021 by Aulart, he has shared the stage with renowned artists such as
Marco Carola, Ilario Alicante, Seth Troxler, Dennis Cruz, Paco Osuna, Kevin Saunderson, Matthias Tanzmann, Sébastien Léger, Matador, among others.
