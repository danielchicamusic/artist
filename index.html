BIO

Telematics Engineer and a true Dancefloor artist. Based in Madrid (ESP) but originally from Manizales (COL), he has built a solid career in the electronic music scene since his beginnings in 2011 as a DJ.
His sound masterfully blends elements of Tech, Minimal House, and Dance Music, delivering high-energy hybrid sets specially designed for clubs and festivals.

He has performed the art of the Warm Up for Marco Carola, Ilario Alicante, Seth Troxler, Sebastien Leger, Dennis Ferrer, Matador, Matthias Tanzmann, Alexis Cabrera, Stefano Noferini, wAFF, Yaya, Paco Osuna, Kevin Saunderson, Marco Faraone, Dennis Cruz, Stacey Pullen, Alexi Delano, Marko Nastic, Jean Pierre & Jesse Caloso, Rafa Barrios, Hector Couto, Pablo Luque, among others.
Thanks to collectives such as Open Doors Techno & House, Techno Night Collective, Scene, ALIB, Booth and Real People.



His music productions have had a significant impact in the United Kingdom, Netherlands, Spain, and Colombia, where his releases have been well received by the local scenes and international labels. Achieving rankings within the Top 100, Top 10, and Top 5 on Beatport.

He was recognised in 2021 through the Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp by Aulart, which marked the beginning of his international journey.
He expanded his musical project during the summers of 2022 and 2023, sharing his music in Ibiza, Madrid, and Barcelona, cities that have deeply influenced the evolution of his sound and artistic career.
