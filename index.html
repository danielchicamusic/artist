Biography
Biografía

Daniel, Telematics Engineer and a true dance floor artist. Based in Madrid (ESP) but originally from Manizales (COL), he has built a solid career in the electronic music scene since his beginnings in 2011. His sound masterfully combines elements of Minimal, House, Tech and Dance Music, delivering high-energy hybrid sets specially designed for clubs and festivals.

Daniel, Ingeniero en Telemática y un verdadero artista de pista de baile. Radicado en Madrid (ESP) pero originario de Manizales (COL), ha construido una sólida carrera en la escena de la música electrónica desde sus inicios en 2011. Su sonido combina magistralmente elementos de Minimal, House, Tech y Dance Music, ofreciendo sets híbridos de alta energía especialmente diseñados para clubs y festivales.

Their productions have had a strong impact in Germany, United Kingdom, Netherlands, Spain, Italy and Colombia, reaching the Top 100, Top 10 and Top 5 on Beatport.

Sus producciones han tenido un fuerte impacto en Alemania, Reino Unido, Países Bajos, España, Italia y Colombia, alcanzando el Top 100, Top 10 y Top 5 en Beatport.

In 2021, recognized by the Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp by Aulart, marking the beginning of his international career where he was later able to take his sound to the decks of Ibiza, Barcelona, Madrid.

En 2021, reconocido por el Amnesia Ibiza Bootcamp by Aulart, marcando el inicio de su carrera internacional, donde posteriormente llevó su sonido a las cabinas de Ibiza, Barcelona y Madrid.

In Colombia he consolidated his career with great brands and promoters such as Open Doors Techno & House, Techno Night Collective, Station Group, SCENE, ALIB, Booth, Real People, Grood Taste, After Mood, MadRadio, MeLosTomo, ILOVEMIERCOLES among others.

En Colombia consolidó su carrera con grandes marcas y promotores como Open Doors Techno & House, Techno Night Collective, Station Group, SCENE, ALIB, Booth, Real People, Grood Taste, After Mood, MadRadio, MeLosTomo, ILOVEMIERCOLES entre otros.
